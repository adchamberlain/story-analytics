How it works

Three steps from data to dashboard.

1

Connect your data

Upload a CSV, paste data, link a Google Sheet, or connect to Snowflake, PostgreSQL, or BigQuery. Data is ingested into a local DuckDB engine for fast queries.

2

Build your charts

Use the visual editor to pick chart types, map columns, and style your output. Write custom SQL when you need more control. The AI assistant can help with queries and formatting.

3

Assemble and share

Drag charts onto a dashboard, arrange the layout, and export. Download as SVG, PNG, PDF, PowerPoint, or a portable HTML file you can host anywhere.
